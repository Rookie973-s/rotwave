About Us

Founded in 2022, The Wave began humbly on WhatsApp — a small space where entertainment lovers could share exciting updates and creative ideas. As our passion and community grew, we expanded fully into Telegram, creating a broader platform for engagement.

Our mission is to revive the greatness of entertainment that feels lost — and make it even better. Driven by an obsession for improvement, we constantly push boundaries to deliver content that blends entertainment and enlightenment.

As software programmers, we thought — why not mix technology with our love for entertainment and create a platform that brings it all together? That’s how this site was born: a digital home where creativity, storytelling, and innovation meet.

From WaveNews to WaveMovies and WaveComics, we’re dedicated to bringing fresh, exciting, and inspiring updates to everyone who loves stories, creativity, and innovation.

The Wave is proudly led by Richfield Ogunlana.
